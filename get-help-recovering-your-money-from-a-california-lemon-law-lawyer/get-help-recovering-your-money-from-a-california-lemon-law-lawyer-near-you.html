    What Are Lemon Laws?

    Lemon laws are created to guard consumers who have bought or leased a faulty product. A manufacturer that cannot fix a vehicle or a product must either repair it or provide a full refund. Lemon laws vary by state, with some only covering used vehicles and others apply only to new cars. There are also some principles guiding lemon laws, such as that consumers have a limited amount of time to file a complaint.
