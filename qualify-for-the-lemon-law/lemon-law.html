What Is The California Lemon Law Time Limit?


The statute of limitations regarding a California Lemon Law claim is four years. The time limit starts when the consumer first experienced warrantable problems with their vehicle. If a person misses the deadline, they will be barred from pursuing a claim and may have to go through arbitration instead.
